Our Motto

Great Work. For Great People. By Great People.

Oxford Communications is a team of people helping people. Our team of strategists, designers, writers, and developers all work together to help our clients reach their goals and share in their success. Through combinations of brand strategy, print and digital design, social media , media, and web we push our clients to new heights. And we love every second.

An Office with Space

Welcome to Lambertville.

Less than 90 minutes from Manhattan and Philly, Oxford is known for its fast pace — without the white-knuckle commute. Outside, however, our Music Mountain headquarters overlooks gardens, woods and the charming town of Lambertville. Some call it “East Coast meets West Coast.” We call it quality of work life. Our Culture Club celebrates it with a monthly agency breakfast, after-hours gatherings, plus special events like our Thanksgiving feast, holiday party, summer BBQ and the annual Oxford Olympics.
