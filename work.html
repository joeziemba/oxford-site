Dossier Systems Inc

Branding & Web Design

Dossier Systems, Inc. – a leading provider of fleet maintenance software – was one of our largest website redesign projects to date. Developed as part of the company’s rebranding from Arsenault Associates to Dossier Systems Inc., the site needed to reflect a new, modern technology brand with content and functionality for both current customers and new sales prospects.

The Redesigned DossierSystemsInc.com

The site we were tasked with replacing relied heavily on outdated Flash technology. Duplicate content, redundant navigation, conflicting calls-to-action, and no clear informational hierarchy created a heavily watered-down user experience caused by years without any content governance or web strategy in place. The company’s employees had very limited ability to add and modify content on their own, making site maintenance difficult.

Dossier Systems Logo

Oxford and our development partner were able to produce a modern, mobile-responsive website with a clear goal-oriented user experience to drive sales leads for the company. The site communicates the features of Dossier’s software platform clearly in a format that encourages user exploration and provides an easy method for user to contact Dossier. A section dedicated to the industries Dossier serves helps users who are unsure if Dossier is right for them identify with the company’s solutions and provides testimonials from customers in that vertical.

In accordance with our client’s requirements, we integrated a password-protected member’s only section with content relevant to users who are already customers of Dossier, an Events calendar, company details, and a blog. The Wordpress CMS served as the base of the site, providing better back-end management for Dossier’s internal teams along with external membership account capabilities.
